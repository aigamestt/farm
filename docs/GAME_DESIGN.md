# Game Design

## Pitch

Sproutstead is a compact survival farming game. The player wakes beside a small camp, turns wild land into crop plots, gathers resources, and survives night pressure from slimes.

## Player Goals

- Build a stable food source by farming crops.
- Manage hunger and energy.
- Collect wood and stone for future crafting.
- Gather enough resources to build the sanctuary and make the valley safe.

## Current Features

- Top-down tile world with generated pixel-art textures.
- Movement, collision-like interaction ranges, camera follow, and HUD.
- Five tools: hoe, watering can, axe, pickaxe, seed pouch.
- Farming loop: till, plant, water, grow, harvest.
- Survival loop: hunger drains over time, energy is spent by tool use, crops restore hunger.
- Resource loop: chop trees, mine rocks, pick berries.
- Day/night cycle with hostile slimes that become active at night.
- Three-hit axe combat with slime-core drops.
- Sanctuary victory recipe: 12 wood, 8 stone, 6 crops, and 2 slime cores.
- Automatic local saves with refresh recovery.

## Future Features

- Stamina recovery through sleep.
- Building placement.
- Multiple seed types.
- Larger map generation and biomes.
