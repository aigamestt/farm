# Milestones

## Milestone 1: Playable Prototype

- Player movement.
- Farming tiles.
- Resource gathering.
- Hunger and energy.
- Day/night loop.
- Basic enemy pressure.

Status: complete.

## Milestone 2: Persistence and Crafting

- Save/load local game state.
- Crafting recipes for fences, campfire, and storage chest.
- Sleep to advance day and recover energy.

## Milestone 3: World Expansion

- Larger generated map.
- Water, grassland, forest, and rocky patches.
- Multiple crop types with different growth times.

## Milestone 4: Polish

- Real pixel-art spritesheets.
- Sound effects and music.
- Main menu and pause screen.
- Better enemy pathing and feedback.
